Your questions answered

What does ROSHARO do?

ROSHARO is a platform to connect the needy to the volunteers with NGO's as a medium. In today's busy world, it is very difficult for a volunteer who wants to help at a particular time in his vicinity to use his service. On the other hand, there are a many people who are in dire need of help. ROSHARO steps in this scenario, and allows a seamless delivery of service by the volunteers to the needy with its exclusive website.

How can you help?

Just as the saying goes "Service to man is service to God". If you have spare time and a skill set that can uplift the needy from their deprived situations, you can volunteer. Just register on our site and list in your preferable timings. We will provide you the suitable places where you can volunteer according to your preferred timimgs and in your vicinity.
